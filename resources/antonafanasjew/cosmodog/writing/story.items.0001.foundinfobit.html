That's strange!

You could swear, you just saw a little transparent reddish pyramide floating in the air ten meters in front of you.

No - it is not really visible, you just FEEL its presence nearby.

It's like a knowledge of something - projected on an imaginary geometric figure.

What the...
Your first urge is to walk away, but somehow you feel an attraction towards the invisible red thing.

You slowly walk to its location and stretch out your arm touching the intangible edge of the pyramide.

Then, something happens.
You can't explain it.

It feels like having lost ones physical properties for a moment. As if the whole universe would slightly shift and then jump back again. In this one moment you can smell colors and touch thoughts.

A second later, you feel your weight again and hear the frequent rhythm of your heart.

The invisible pyramide presence has disappeared.

The world is still there and nothing has changed.

No, that's not true.

There was a change.

You KNOW something now.

It feels like a new focus in the far corner of your mind on something that you always knew.

It's like realizing the presence of the TV through the sudden silence after it was switched off.

You have learned something important.

You have no idea what it was.
