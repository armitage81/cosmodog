For a long time you search for traces of a mysterious alien race.

Finally, you hear rumors about ancient monolyths on the surfase of an abandoned planet.

On the way there, your ship Cosmodog is hit by a missile fired from an automated guardian satellite.

Cosmodog is destroyed. You manage to reach the escape pod and land on the surface.
Find 20 alien monolyths, reveal the mystery around the aliens and escape the planet.
